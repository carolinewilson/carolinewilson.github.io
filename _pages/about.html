Ambitious and driven, in December 2018 I embarked upon the greatest challenge of my life; rowing 3000 miles across the Atlantic to challenge the everyday use of plastic as part of team Status Row.

After returning to the UK in February 2019 I took on a new challenge to manage the 3 month Kik-Plastic tour (7000km cycle participating in daily beach cleans with local communities) further raising awareness of the plastic pollution crisis and activating communities along the way.

Always looking for the next challenge, I'm passionate, determined and highly motivated to drive change for the better. Working with great teams to solve real-life problems, and creating a few along the way.
